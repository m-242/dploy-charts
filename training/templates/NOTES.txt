Training Environment Deployed!
==============================

{{- if .Values.ingress.enabled }}
Access your training environment at:
  https://{{ .Values.ingressHost }}/
{{- else }}
To access your training environment, run:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "training.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

Then open http://localhost:8080 in your browser.
{{- end }}

Components:
-----------
  - Training UI: Serves instructions, terminal (xterm.js), and check API
  - Shell: Your sandbox environment

Tips:
-----
  - Navigate between steps using Previous/Next buttons or arrow keys
  - Click "Check" to verify your progress on each step
  - Use Ctrl+Enter to quickly run a check
  - Resize the panels by dragging the divider
